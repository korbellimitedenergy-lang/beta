Safety Inspections & Compliance

We provide thorough forklift safety inspections to ensure your equipment meets OSHA standards and operates safely in your workplace. Our detailed reports and compliance checks help reduce liability, prevent accidents, and keep your operation running without interruptions.

We Fix What Keeps You Lifting.

These are just some of the services we offer at Northview Forklift & Repair.

Hydraulic Repair

Our hydraulic repair services address leaks, pressure issues, and component failures to keep your forklift operating smoothly and safely. From hoses and cylinders to pumps and valves, we provide reliable repairs that restore performance and prevent costly downtime.

Forklift Diagnostics & Troubleshooting

Our forklift diagnostics and troubleshooting services quickly identify mechanical, electrical, and hydraulic issues to get to the root of the problem. Using advanced tools and experienced technicians, we deliver accurate repairs that reduce downtime and prevent recurring issues.

Mobile Services

Our mobile forklift repair service brings expert technicians directly to your location, minimizing downtime and keeping your operations moving. We provide fast, reliable on-site diagnostics and repairs so you can get back to work without the hassle of transporting equipment.

Preventative Maintenance

We help you maintain your equipment and provide regular inspections, such as checking fluid levels, tires, brakes, hydraulics and electrical systems. We help identify wear and potential issues before they lead to costly breakdowns and repairs. Ultimately extending the life of your equipment, reduce downtime and ensure compliance and safety standards.
