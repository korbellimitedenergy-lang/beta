Services

Here at Northview Forklift Repair your equipment is our priority.

Forklift Maintenance & Repair

We will keep your equipment running safely and efficiently. Regular inspections, fluid checks, and component servicing help prevent costly breakdowns and extend the life of your machines.

Material Handling Equipment & Scissor Lifts

From pallet jacks to reach trucks, we keep your equipment moving efficiently with reliable service and support along with scissor lift maintenance and repair ensures safe lifting performance and compliance with safety standards. From hydraulic systems to electrical components, we keep your lifts reliable and job-site ready.

Tire Services

Equipped with a 200-ton tire press and a wide range of OEM hubs in stock, we handle forklift tire replacements quickly and efficiently—reducing downtime and keeping your operation running without interruption.

Battery Services

Our forklift battery services include thorough testing, reliable repairs, and Xtender solutions to prolong the life of your industrial batteries and maintain consistent performance in demanding environments.

On-Site Mobile Lift Services

Our fully equipped mobile service units are ready to diagnose and repair your equipment right on-site—minimizing disruption and getting your lifts back in operation fast. From emergency breakdowns to routine service calls, we deliver shop-quality work wherever you need it, in a timely manner.

Preventative Maintenance & Inspections

Stay ahead of costly failures with scheduled maintenance you can rely on. Our comprehensive inspections catch issues early, extend equipment life, and keep your lifts operating safely and efficiently. We don’t just check boxes—we protect your investment and your uptime.

Custom Modifications & Cosmetic Repair

Need your equipment to do more—or look better doing it? We offer tailored modifications to fit your operation, along with professional cosmetic repairs that restore your fleet’s appearance. From functional upgrades to full refreshes, we make your equipment work smarter and look sharper.

Electrical Diagnostics

Electrical issues can be complex—we make them simple. Using advanced diagnostic tools and real-world experience, we quickly pinpoint faults, wiring issues, and control failures. Whether it’s intermittent problems or complete system outages, we get to the root of it fast.

Hydraulic & Mast Repair

Power and precision start with a solid hydraulic system. We service cylinders, hoses, pumps, and full mast assemblies to keep your equipment lifting smoothly and safely. From leaks to major rebuilds, we handle it all with precision and reliability.

Fleet Management & Maintenance Schedules

Take control of your entire fleet with structured maintenance planning. We help you track service intervals, and keep every piece of equipment performing at its best. Smart scheduling, detailed records, and proactive service—so nothing gets overlooked.
